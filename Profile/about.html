About Me

        I am a driven, enthusiastic, fun-loving guy with a passion for anything high tech. I was born in Ontario, Canada and moved to Virgina with my parents when I was 10. Since I was a child playing with my Nintendo, and various other consoles, I have had an interest in how things work. My parents would buy me some new gadget and within a day I would have it taken apart and had made an attempt to put it back together. I was very lucky to have access to a computer when I was very young and was exposed to new technology on a regular basis.

        I very recently moved to the Redmond, WA area to start my career in programming and web development. I hope to meet some like minded people through various meet-up groups to help in my development process and to hopefully play music with.

        I am a music lover at heart, I love to play, write and listen to many types and genres of music. I was in a band in Florida for 5 years called Machine Gun Funk. We toured around Florida and the neighboring states for a few years and put out two records, one was a self titled live EP and the other was called "Homeland Obscurity". We recorded with an excellent producer, Kevin Pandele, at Damage Room Studios. I decided to leave the band to be closer to my family in Maine and help them through a difficult time but I hope to find some talented people to work with here in Seattle.
